Surat dari Pendiri Perusahaan

Our dedication to become the best ice cream manufacturer that always maintains its quality by using natural ingredients and prioritizing customer satisfaction has succeeded in bringing us to a long journey filled with various obstacles. Until now PT. Campina Ice Cream Industry Tbk is proven to be able continuously growth and provide the best products to all families in Indonesia.

This family business which was started from the small house in Surabaya, has now grown rapidly to become one of the largest local ice cream manufacturer in Indonesia. We continuously innovate to provide satisfaction and fulfill desire of our consumer. Our commitment to be the best is realized by maintaining the quality taste of our various favorite product that are always liked by all levels of society.
